Welcome to our rubber recycling company, I and S. We specialize in recycling rubber materials and converting them into high-quality products that can be used again. Our mission is to provide a sustainable solution to rubber waste while also contributing to a cleaner environment.

At I and S, we believe in transparency and honesty in all our operations. We take pride in our commitment to providing environmentally friendly solutions and reducing the amount of rubber waste that ends up in landfills. We use advanced technologies and sustainable practices to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our team of experts has years of experience in the rubber recycling industry and is dedicated to providing our clients with the best possible service. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients and working together to achieve our shared goals.

Our Vision

We have a clear vision for the future of rubber recycling. We are committed to expanding our operations and taking our business to the next level, by investing in new technologies and expanding our product offerings.

Our long-term goal is to become a leading provider of high-quality, recycled rubber products to a range of industries, including construction, transportation, and manufacturing. To achieve this goal, we have developed a phased approach to our business development, which will allow us to build on our strengths and take advantage of new opportunities as they arise.

If you share our vision for a sustainable future and are interested in working with us, please get in touch. We would be happy to discuss our plans and explore potential collaborations.
