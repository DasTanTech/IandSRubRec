Our Recycling Facility

The Heart of Sustainable Manufacturing

Our tire pyrolysis plant employs advanced technology to transform discarded tires into valuable resources.Through advanced machinery and innovative techniques, we extract valuable materials from the tires, resulting in two primary outputs- rubber mulch and clean steel.

The tire shreds are efficiently processed to produce high-quality rubber mulch. This rubber mulch finds wide applications in landscaping, playground surfaces, and various other industries. It offers exceptional durability, safety, and sustainability, providing an eco-friendly alternative to traditional landscaping materials.

In addition to rubber mulch, our recycling plant also recovers clean steel from the tire recycling process. The steel components of the tires are separated and undergo rigorous cleaning to ensure their purity and quality. This clean steel is then prepared for reuse in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and automotive.

By utilizing tire pyrolysis, we contribute to the circular economy by repurposing rubber waste and transforming it into valuable materials. Our tire pyrolysis plant is dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and creating a greener future by revolutionizing the recycling of tires.

Breaking Boundaries with Pyrolysis

Sustainable Conversion of Tyre Shreds to Rubber Crumb and Pristine Steel

At I & S Rubber Recycling,we are dedicated to creating sustainable solutions for rubber waste. We have developed a revolutionary pyrolysis process that allows us to transform rubber waste into high-quality products without harming the environment. Pyrolysis is a thermal process that breaks down rubber waste into smaller molecules, which can then be used to create a range of valuable products.

Our team of experts has perfected the pyrolysis process to ensure maximum efficiency and minimal waste. We are committed to utilizing the latest technologies to reduce our environmental impact and create a more sustainable future.
