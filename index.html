We Buy Tyre Shreds with Embedded Steel

I & S Rubber Recycling Firm is committed to reducing waste in landfills and promoting sustainability by repurposing rubber waste. We currently buy two types of products for recycling:

- Tyre Shred with Embedded Steel of 50x100mm

- Tyre Shred with Embedded Steel of 100x100mm

These shreds are created by shredding discarded tires and removing any impurities, resulting in a high-quality product that can be repurposed for a variety of applications.

The Tyre Shreds with Embedded Steel of 50x100mm are ideal for use in construction and landscaping, as they can provide drainage and stability to surfaces. The larger Tyre Shreds with Embedded Steel of 100x100mm are commonly used in civil engineering and as a substitute for gravel in road construction.

At I & S Rubber Recycling, we are dedicated to promoting sustainability and reducing waste. By buying these rubber products and repurposing them for other applications, we are contributing to a more eco-friendly future.

Revolutionary Pyrolysis Process

At I & S Rubber Recycling,we are dedicated to creating sustainable solutions for rubber waste. We have developed a revolutionary pyrolysis process that allows us to transform rubber waste into high-quality products without harming the environment. Pyrolysis is a thermal process that breaks down rubber waste into smaller molecules, which can then be used to create a range of valuable products.

Our team of experts has perfected the pyrolysis process to ensure maximum efficiency and minimal waste. We are committed to utilizing the latest technologies to reduce our environmental impact and create a more sustainable future. By choosing I & S Rubber Recycling, you can trust that your rubber waste is being recycled responsibly and with the utmost care.

Visit our Science page to learn more about the pyrolysis process and how we are leading the way in sustainable rubber recycling. We believe that our commitment to innovation and sustainability is what sets us apart, and we are proud to be pioneers in the industry.
